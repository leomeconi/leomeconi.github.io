About Us

The LOAD is an Italian band founded by Leo Meconi and Santo during their high school years. Since early 2023, they have been performing across venues in Italy, later completing the lineup with Matras on electric guitar. Their sound blends rock, pop, and singer-songwriter influences, delivering engaging and dynamic live performances. Their shows focus primarily on original songs, enriched by a selection of covers that reflect the band’s artistic roots and inspirations. Frontman Leo Meconi has already released three albums as a solo artist. His latest work, Heaven (November 2024), was produced and co-written by Don Mescall, a multi-platinum producer renowned for collaborations with some of the most influential international artists of the ’90s and 2000s.
